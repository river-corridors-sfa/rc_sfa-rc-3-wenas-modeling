Intro and scope:

Since there are issues with the original code I wrote, Im going to rewrite in RMD form so itll be easier to explain what Im doing and diagnose where things are going wrong. The goal is to take the output files from modeling and plot how different scenarios impact water quantity (flow) and quality (concentrations of sediments, NO3, and DOC). There are two scenario types: fire characteristics (manipulate fire intensity/burn extent) and watershed characteristics (manipulate watershed slope / flows / vegetation). Currently, fire characteristics are available for the New model, so will plot those, then add in watershed characteristics once model outputs are uploaded.
